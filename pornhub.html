Giới thiệu món ăn: Phở Việt Nam

Pho VietNam

Pho is a traditional Vietnamese dish. It includes a bowl of delicious, soft pho broth, accompanied by meats, vegetables and spices.

Pho is served hot, usually during breakfast or dinner, and is often eaten with raw vegetables, bean sprouts, lime and chili.

This is a delicious and popular dish not only in Vietnam but also highly appreciated around the world.
